Impact Equality Demo

Impact equality™ is a fully automated online toolkit that enables you to complete your equality impact assessments in a very user-friendly way with no fuss.  Not only does the system ensure that you comply fully with equality legislation, but users of the system have also told us how they have enjoyed seeing genuine improvements to their activity and departments.

But before you can start to reap the benefits of the system within your organisation, you need to gain access to the Impact Equality™ toolkit and you can do that today for free.

After completing a very simple registration process, you will be walked step-by-step through the assessment process, where you will be prompted with a series of questions.

Impact Equality™ has been built and extensively tested with the user in mind.  The questions are written in plain English and each section is tracked, enabling you to clearly see which sections are complete and those that are still to complete.  This flexibility means that you can start your assessment today and complete or come back to it at a later date if you need to.

Handy Report Function

The toolkit also comes with a very handy report function, which enables you to create a customised and easy to follow report based on your responses - at the touch of a button.  No complicated analysis required.
