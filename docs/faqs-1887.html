FAQs

What sort of things can Impact Equality do for me?


The amount that Impact Equality can do for you depends upon whether you have the free version or the full version.  The free version provides at a basic level the ability to automate the process of completing equality impact assessments online step-by-step.  The full version of Impact Equality is a fully automated online system to manage, monitor and undertake equality impact assessments with a centralised adminstrative system.

How much does it cost?

You can take advantage of Impact Equality today by accessing the free version.  Although the system has limited functionality, it gives you a test-drive of the system and enables you to fully undertake a equality impact assessment. The full version is available on an annual subscription basis.

 Can I try Impact Equality?

You can try the basic features of Impact Equality instantly for free.  If you would like to arrange for a free no obligation consultation meeting on the full version, you may call our team today on 0845 226 0363. 

How does the system allow for the differences in my organisation?

Organisations that need to comply with the completion of equality impact assessments must follow a series of steps to demonstrate compliance.  The system has been fully developed and is updated to ensure full compliance to legislation.  With the full version of Impact Equality, we do factor in that your organisation may have different needs and we will work with you to identify and meet your organisational needs at the outset to ensure optimal integration.

Who is Impact Equality provided by?

Impact Equality has been supported and developed by a group of consultants at Black Radley with many years of experience of working within the public sector.

How do I get started?

Just signup today for your free access or give our team a call on 0845 226 0363 to ask any questions or arrange a no obligation consultation meeting.

What are the technical requirements?

Impactequality™ is an online application which can be accessed by any staff that have access to the Internet. Users receive access keys by way of email, initiated by the management of the impact assessment process.

This configuration enables a fast and secure implementation without the need for extensive involvement of IT departments.

Impactequality™ runs on Internet Explorer 6 and 7 and Firefox 2 and 3. Adobe pdf reader and Flash are also required.

Impactequality™ is hosted in the UK with guaranteed connectivity and response times.

The standing data such as Directorate structure and corporate objectives will be provided by the customer for updating by Black Radley Systems Ltd.
