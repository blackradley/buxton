Cohesion Equalities Performance Tool

CEPT is a new online product which will:

  • assist organisations with the gateway or screening process;
  • assist organisations to mainstream equalities activity as required by legislation;
  • enable organisations to benchmark their equalities activity across the sector;
  • encourage frontline delivery staff to consider how their service affects members of different equalities groups;
  • provide a baseline measure of the organisation's impact on community cohesion.
